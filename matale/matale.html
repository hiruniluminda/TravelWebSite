WELCOME TO MATALE DISTRICT

Matale Administrative District is an administrative district in Sri Lanka. It is believed that the center of the island of Ceylon is in the Matale administrative district. Its size is in square kilometers. 1993.9.
We welcome you to find your unique Matale district travel experience.

Knuckles Mountain Range

The Knuckles Forest covers an area of 21,000 hectares in the Kandy and Matale Districts of the Central Province of Sri Lanka. The high mountains are almost always covered with fog. The western slope here is covered with battalions.

Meemure

Meemure village is located in the Knuckles mountain range between Kandy and Matale districts. Meemure, with a population of 400, is one of the most remote villages in Sri Lanka. It is accessible only through a 14 km trail starting from Lulwatta which makes it a unique and exciting tour. Diyakarella, Ella Falls and Lakegala Kanda are some of the attractions of Meemure.

Wasgamuwa National Park

The park also has a dry zone and a wet zone climate. Therefore, this park has a special place among the national parks in this country. Therefore, Wasgamuwa National Park is a place rich in biodiversity. The park is bordered by the Parakrama Samudra to the north, the Mahaweli River to the east, the Dunuwila Oya to the south and the Kalu Ganga and Amban rivers to the west.
Wasgamuwa National Park is famous as the tallest elephant sanctuary in Sri Lanka. It is spread over an area of about 40,000 hectares.

Aluvihare Rock Temple

The Alu Vihara can be reached by traveling about 1/2 km from Matale towards Dambulla on the Kandy-Anuradhapura road. According to information received from the surrounding areas, 18 caves have been discovered during the excavations at the Aluvihara and 8 more caves have been found in the nearby Biridewela mountain range. This temple also contains paintings and sculptures of the Kandy tradition of the temples built during the Kandyan period with letterless drip caves. The temple has an ancient stupa, a Bodhi tree and a collection of valuable books.

Sembuwatta Lake

The natural spring water lake, the natural swimming pool by the lake, the Campbell's Lane Forest Reserve, the tea plantations and the temperate climate all attract tourists.

Nalanda Gedige

It is an ancient complete stone temple located near Matale in Sri Lanka and its original location is considered to be the center of Sri Lanka. The building was built between the 8th and 10th centuries with Tamil architecture and is believed to have been used by Buddhists.
