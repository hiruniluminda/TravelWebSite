WELCOME TO PUTTALAM DISTRICT

The total land area of Puttalam district is 3,072 square kilometers. Puttalam is famous for its beautiful lagoon. The shallow sea is also a haven for fishermen. There is also a salt pans in Puttalam.
We welcome you to find your unique Puttalam district travel experience.

Kalpitiya Diving Center

Kalpitiya Diving Center is a popular name among tourists as well as locals. Kalpitiya is a lagoon with a total land area of 16.73 km. The lagoon consists of 14 islands, which allow for a variety of activities in addition to the usual scenery. It also offers ample space for adventurous water sports activities. Rich in various fish species, this place has truly become one of the premier dry fish production areas in Sri Lanka.

Munneshwaram Hindu Kovil

Munneswaram Kovil is an important Hindu religious site located in Chilaw, Sri Lanka. Built for Lord Shiva, this temple is one of the five ancient Hindu shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva in the South Indian region.

Vilpaththu National Park

Located in the northwestern lowland dry zone of Sri Lanka, the park covers an area of 1,317 sq km and ranges from 0 to 152 m above sea level. It is the oldest national park.
