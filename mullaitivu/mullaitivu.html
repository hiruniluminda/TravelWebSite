WELCOME TO MULLAITIVU DISTRICT

Mullaitivu district is spread over an area of 2617 square kilometers. It also had a population of 91947 as of 2001.
We welcome you to find your unique Mullaitivu district travel experience.

Mullaitivu Beach

It is a beautiful beach located on the northeastern coast of Sri Lanka, providing a calm and tropical environment. Mullaitivu is famous for its large fishing settlements, which reveal the true beauty of Mullaitivu and enjoy the sun and the sea.

Muththu Iyankattu kulam

Reconstruction of the tank with a catchment area of 171 km2 began in 1959. By the late 1960s, the bund was 5,850 feet high and 27 feet high, with a storage capacity of 41,000 acres and a water distribution of 3,1005 acres. The left and right bank sluices were 3 feet 3 inches by 4 inches each. By 2014, the lake was able to irrigate 6,100 acres.

Nayaru Beach
