WELCOME TO GAMPAHA DISTRICT

Gampaha District is a district in the Western Province of Sri Lanka. The total land area of ​​the district is 1,386.6 sq km and the total population is 2,294,641. The district capital is Gampaha and the largest city in the district is Negombo. Other prominent towns include Ja-Ela, Katana, Mirigama, Wattala and etc.
We welcome you to find your unique Gampaha district travel experience.

Guruge National Park

Guruge Nature Park is the first theme park in Sri Lanka and has a glorious history mixed with fun water fun. The tour will undoubtedly be an unforgettable experience to see the proud cultural, traditional and historical heritage as well as the places of entertainment. Future attractions of the park include a gem museum, a unique butterfly and insect habitat and a wave pool to add to the rich experience of this theme park.

Henarathgoda Botanic Gardens

Gampaha Botanic Gardens, also known as Henarathgoda Botanic Gardens, is located on the Gampaha-Minuwangoda main road, about 450 meters from the Gampaha railway station. The park is home to a large variety of plants, many of which cover every corner of the tropical climate and cover an area of 43 acres.

Maligatenna Dageba

Although close to Gampaha and close to the A1 main road from Colombo to Kandy, the Siyane Korale archeological sites are seldom visited by foreigners as they are not mentioned in the general pocket guides. Maligathenna is the highest mountain in the Gampaha District. The Maligathenna main temple complex consists of two terraces called the lower courtyard and the upper courtyard. There is a stupa and an ancient Bo tree on the top of the Maligathenna hill.

Aththanagalla Buddhist Temple

Attanagalla Rajamaha Viharaya is one of the most famous ancient royal temples in Sri Lanka. It is unique in that it is the only ancient temple in history to have a Bodhisattva named as the place where he performed his almsgiving. He sacrificed his life in gratitude for a privilege he had received from a poor ordinary citizen.

Splatter Painball

Only Paintball location in Srilanka where you can get to play inside a jungle. Friendly staff, awesome food, camping also available if you are interested. Great place to have fun with your friends and colleagues.

Negombo Beach

The beach and the fishing industry make this place popular among visitors. Tourists who want to go on an adventure trip can definitely visit this place. Residents of the coastal area sell batik and handicrafts. You can also see coral reefs around the beach. Divers will love to visit this beach.
