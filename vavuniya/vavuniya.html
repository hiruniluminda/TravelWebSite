WELCOME TO VAVUNIYA DISTRICT

Vavuniya District is one of the 25 districts of Sri Lanka and is the second tier administrative division of the country. The capital of the district is Vavuniya.
We welcome you to find your unique Vavuniya district travel experience.

Madukanda Viharaya

Madukanda Temple is a historical Buddhist temple. Madukanda village is located 4 km from Vavuniya and is a historical religious site. The Madukanda temple is easily accessible. In addition, the temple provides free accommodation and basic amenities to visitors to the Madukanda Temple.

Kandasami Kovil

The great and golden Nallur Kandaswamy Temple has long been a symbol of the region for people of all faiths. This temple has a rich history which contributes significantly to the unique Northern Heritage of our island and also hosts the annual Nallur Festival in the month of August every year. The 25-day festival is the longest in Sri Lanka and is a colorful month of parades, dances, drumming and much more.

Archeological Museum Of Vavuniya

Vavuniya Archaeological Museum is located in Vavuniya, North Central Sri Lanka. The museum displays objects of archaeological significance in and around Vavuniya. Some of these exhibits include Buddha statues, statues of Hindu deities and some antiquities dating back to the 5th and 8th centuries
