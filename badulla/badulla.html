WELCOME TO BADULLA DISTRICT

Badulla District is a district in the Uva Province of Sri Lanka. The total land area of the district is 2,861 sq km and the total population is 837,000.
We welcome you to find your unique Badulla district travel experience.

Ella Rock

It is the most famous place in Badulla and is a climbing route near the Ella Railway Station which leads to the top of the Ella Rock. The trek to Ella Rock will take you a long way to reach the top of the hill but you will never get tired.

Little Adams Peak

Little Adam's mountaineering in Sri Lanka has a marked road leading to the top with many signposts, so you can easily climb the mountain without a guide. The top of the hill should not be confused with the top of Adam's hill, it is a short distance and a considerably steep and long climb. The mountain derives its name from the peak of Adam, as it recreates its shape in a small form.

Nine Arches Bridge

The Nine Arch Bridge is a viaduct bridge in Sri Lanka. It is one of the best examples of colonial era railway construction in the country.

Ravana Falls

Ravana Falls is a beautiful waterfall located in Sri Lanka. The total height is 25m and the altitude is 1050m. It is also formed by a tributary of the Kiridi Oya.

Diyaluma Falls

Diyaluma Falls is the third highest waterfall in Sri Lanka at 171m and the 361st highest in the world. The waterfall is created from the Poonagala Oya.

Lipton's Seat

Lipton's seat is an invisible destination in Sri Lanka. Handapanagala Lake, Chandrika Lake, Uda Valwewa Lake, Vedda Hill Mountain Range as well as the Port of Hambantota located in the southern coastal areas of Sri Lanka and in five provinces can be seen prominently.

Muthiyangana Temple

Muthiyanganaya Kali is one of the sixteen places of worship. The features of this land are the temple entrance pandals, the stupa, the temple hall, the octopus Bodhi tree, the entrance pandals with two peacock statues on either side of a concentric Buddha image. Apart from this, here is an ancient petroglyph depicting stone tablets, ancient stone pillars and the Wessanthara Jataka.

Madulsima

Madolsima is a very scenic area. The beauty of the surroundings is indescribable. There are several ways to access the Little Doomsday.

Adisham Bungalow

Adisham is a large bungalow that is today maintained as a Roman Catholic Benedictine monastery. Here the priests make various products from the fruits of the bungalow garden and release them to the market.
